<p><p>The interventional cardiology market encompasses various devices that aid in diagnosing and treating cardiovascular diseases. Cardiology catheters are used for delivering treatments and diagnostics. Guidewires assist in navigating through blood vessels during procedures. Balloon catheters facilitate the dilation of narrowed arteries. Coronary stents provide structural support to keep arteries open. Fractional flow reserve measures blood flow and pressure in coronary arteries to assess blockages, while intravascular ultrasound offers real-time imaging of blood vessels for better procedural guidance.</p></p>
