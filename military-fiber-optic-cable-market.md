<p><strong>The Military Fiber Optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multimode-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military fiber optic cable market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode and multimode fibers. Single-mode cables utilize a single light path for long-distance communication, offering high bandwidth and minimal signal loss, ideal for vast military networks. In contrast, multimode fibers carry multiple light paths, allowing shorter distance transmission with higher data capacity but more signal attenuation. Both types are crucial for secure and reliable military communication, catering to diverse operational requirements in various environments.</p></p>
