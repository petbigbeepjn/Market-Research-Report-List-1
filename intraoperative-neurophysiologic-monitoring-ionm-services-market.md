<p><p>Intraoperative Neurophysiologic Monitoring (IONM) services encompass various market types, including Electroencephalography (EEG), Electromyography (EMG), and Evoked Potentials (EP). EEG monitors electrical activity in the brain, ensuring neural function during surgery. EMG assesses muscle responses to nerve stimulation, providing real-time feedback on motor pathways. EP measures brain responses to sensory stimuli, helping to evaluate the integrity of specific neural pathways. These techniques are crucial for minimizing neurological risks during surgical procedures, enhancing patient safety and surgical outcomes.</p></p>
