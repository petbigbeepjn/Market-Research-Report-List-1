<p><ul><li>Single Point Blood Glucose Meters</li><li>Continuous Glucose Monitoring Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood glucose monitoring market is primarily divided into two types: Single Point Blood Glucose Meters and Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) Systems. Single Point Blood Glucose Meters provide instant readings by testing a drop of blood, offering convenience for occasional checks. In contrast, CGM Systems continuously track glucose levels through a sensor placed under the skin, providing real-time data and trends. This continuous monitoring helps individuals manage diabetes more effectively, facilitating timely adjustments to lifestyle and medication.</p></p>
