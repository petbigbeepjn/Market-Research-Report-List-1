<p><p>Pulpers are essential in the paper market for breaking down raw materials into pulp. The main types include hydrapulper, drum hydrapulper, and broke pulper. Hydrapulpers use a high-speed rotor to efficiently mix and disintegrate raw materials. Drum hydrapulpers function with a cylindrical design, allowing continuous processing of bulk materials. Broke pulpers are specifically designed to recycle paper scraps, known as broke, back into the pulping process. Each type caters to different operational needs in paper production.</p></p>
