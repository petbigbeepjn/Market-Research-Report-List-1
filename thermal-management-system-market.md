<p><strong>The Thermal Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal management system market is categorized into passive and active solutions. Passive systems rely on natural heat dissipation methods, such as conduction, convection, and radiation, using materials like heat sinks or thermal pads to manage temperature without external energy. In contrast, active systems employ mechanical or electrical components, like fans or liquid cooling, to actively control heat levels, allowing for more effective and precise temperature regulation. Both types are essential for optimizing performance in various applications, including electronics and automotive industries.</p></p>
