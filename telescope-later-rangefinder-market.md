<p><ul><li>PhaseMethodTelemeter</li><li>PulseMethodTelemeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Telescope Later Rangefinder market includes two primary types: Phase Method Telemeter and Pulse Method Telemeter. The Phase Method measures distance by analyzing phase shifts in reflected light waves, offering high precision for short to medium ranges. The Pulse Method calculates distance by measuring the time delay of laser pulses, excelling in long-range accuracy. Both methods cater to diverse applications like surveying, military, and sports, with the Phase Method favored for detailed work and the Pulse Method for extended distances.</p></p>
