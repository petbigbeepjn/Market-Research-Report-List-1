<p><p>The slotted brake disc market consists of three main types: ventilated slotted brake discs, perforated brake discs, and slotted brake discs. Ventilated slotted brake discs feature internal air channels to enhance cooling, improving performance under high-stress conditions. Perforated brake discs incorporate holes to reduce weight and increase airflow, providing superior heat dissipation. Standard slotted brake discs have grooves that help with water drainage and debris removal, leading to improved braking efficiency and less fade during heavy usage.</p></p>
