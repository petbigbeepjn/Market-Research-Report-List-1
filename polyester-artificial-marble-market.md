<p><strong>The Polyester Artificial Marble Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pour Forming</li><li>Compression Molding</li><li>Bulk Scrap Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyester Artificial Marble market is categorized into three main types: Pour Forming, Compression Molding, and Bulk Scrap Molding. Pour Forming involves the liquid resin being poured into molds to create intricate designs. Compression Molding employs high pressure to shape pre-mixed materials, resulting in durable finished products. Bulk Scrap Molding utilizes recycled polyester waste to produce new marble-like surfaces, promoting sustainability. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of production efficiency, design flexibility, and environmental responsibility.</p></p>
