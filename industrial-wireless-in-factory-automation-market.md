<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial Wireless in Factory Automation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN)</li><li>Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN)</li><li>Satellite (GNSS)</li><li>Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN)</li><li>Cellular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial wireless in factory automation encompasses various market types. Wireless Local Area Networks (WLAN) facilitate high-speed connections within limited areas, ideal for real-time data exchange. Wireless Personal Area Networks (WPAN) serve short-range communication needs among devices, enhancing connectivity for tools and sensors. Satellite (GNSS) ensures global positioning and tracking for remote systems. Low-Power Wide-Area Networks (LPWAN) support long-range communication with minimal power consumption, suitable for IoT devices. Cellular networks provide robust, extensive coverage, enabling reliable data transmission across larger distances.</p></p>
