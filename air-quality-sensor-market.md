<p><strong>The Air Quality Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Signal-Gas Monitoring Sensor</li><li>Multi-Gas Monitoring Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air quality sensor market features primarily two types: signal-gas monitoring sensors and multi-gas monitoring sensors. Signal-gas monitoring sensors are designed to detect and measure specific gases, providing precise data on particular pollutants such as carbon monoxide or sulfur dioxide. In contrast, multi-gas monitoring sensors can simultaneously detect and analyze multiple gases, offering a broader overview of air quality. This versatility makes multi-gas sensors increasingly vital for comprehensive environmental monitoring and compliance with health standards.</p></p>
