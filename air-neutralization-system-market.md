Neutralization tanks, on the other hand, treat acidic or basic effluents by using chemical reactions to neutralize them before discharge. Both systems play crucial roles in environmental management, ensuring compliance with air quality regulations and minimizing the impact of industrial emissions on the atmosphere.</p></p>
