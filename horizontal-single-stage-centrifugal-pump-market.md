<p><p>The horizontal single-stage centrifugal pump market is segmented into self-priming and non-self-priming centrifugal pumps. Self-priming pumps can draw liquid into the pump from a dry state, making them suitable for applications where the pump may not always be filled with liquid. Non-self-priming pumps require the pump casing to be filled with liquid before initiating operation, typically used in applications with a consistent liquid source. Both types cater to various industries, emphasizing efficiency, reliability, and performance needs.</p></p>
