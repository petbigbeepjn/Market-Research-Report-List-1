<p><ul><li>Francis Turbine</li><li>Kaplan Turbine</li><li>Diagonal Turbine</li><li>Tubular Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydro turbine generator sets are crucial for converting hydro energy into electricity. The Francis turbine is a mixed-flow turbine suitable for medium head sites, characterized by its curved blades that enable high efficiency. Kaplan turbines are designed for low head applications, featuring adjustable blades to optimize performance. Diagonal turbines combine features of Francis and Kaplan turbines, making them versatile for various water flows. Tubular turbines are used in low head sites, with a cylindrical design that minimizes environmental impact while maximizing output.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Hydro Turbine Generator Sets Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Turbines</li><li>Reaction Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydro turbine generator sets market encompasses two primary types: impulse turbines and reaction turbines. Impulse turbines utilize the kinetic energy of water jets to spin the turbine, making them ideal for high-head applications with low flow rates. In contrast, reaction turbines operate by harnessing both the kinetic and potential energy of flowing water, suited for low to medium-head sites with higher flow rates. Together, they serve diverse hydroelectric power generation needs, optimizing efficiency based on site conditions.</p></p>
