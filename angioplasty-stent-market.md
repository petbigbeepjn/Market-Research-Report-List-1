<p><p>The Angioplasty Stent Market is categorized into Type I and Type II segments based on stent characteristics. Type II stents include drug-eluting stents, which are coated with medication to prevent tissue growth and reduce the risk of blockage. This differentiation allows for tailored treatment options depending on patient needs and physician preferences, addressing varying risks and benefits.</p></p>
