<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) refers to techniques that assess materials and components without causing damage. Key market types include ultrasonic testing, which uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws; radiography, employing X-rays or gamma rays for imaging; liquid penetrant testing, which reveals surface defects by applying dye; and magnetic particle testing, used for detecting surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials through magnetic particles. Each method is crucial for ensuring safety and integrity across various industries.</p></p>
