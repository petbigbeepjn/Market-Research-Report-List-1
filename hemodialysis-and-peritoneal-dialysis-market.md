<p><p>Hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis are two types of renal replacement therapies for patients suffering from chronic kidney disease. Hemodialysis involves the use of a machine to filter waste and excess fluids from the blood, typically performed in a clinical setting or at home with the appropriate equipment. In contrast, peritoneal dialysis utilizes the lining of the abdominal cavity (peritoneum) as a natural filter, with a fluid solution introduced into the abdomen to capture waste products; this process can often be managed at home.</p><p>The Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.20% during the forecast period. This growth is attributed to various factors, including the increasing prevalence of kidney disorders, advancements in dialysis technology, and a rising geriatric population. Furthermore, there is a noticeable shift towards home-based dialysis options, providing greater convenience and flexibility for patients. Innovations such as automated peritoneal dialysis and portable hemodialysis devices are also contributing to market expansion, enabling improved patient outcomes and quality of life. The integration of telehealth and remote monitoring technologies is enhancing the management of dialysis, making treatments more accessible and efficient.</p></p>
