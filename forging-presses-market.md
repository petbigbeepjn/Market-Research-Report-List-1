<p><p>The forging presses market is primarily categorized into two types: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical presses utilize a system of gears and flywheels to deliver high-speed punches, making them suitable for high-volume production with quick cycle times. In contrast, hydraulic presses rely on fluid power to exert force, providing greater versatility and control for complex shapes and thicker materials. Both types serve distinct manufacturing needs, driving innovation and efficiency in metal forming processes across various industries.</p></p>
