Adhesive tape is a versatile, pre-applied solution ideal for sealing and bonding in automotive applications, providing a quick and efficient application process. In contrast, adhesive paste offers higher viscosity and is suited for more demanding bonding tasks, such as in structural applications, where durability and strength are crucial. Both types enhance performance in vehicle assembly, improving efficiency and contributing to vehicle longevity.</p></p>
