<p><p>Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) is a method where a product is tightly wrapped in a plastic film, creating a second skin-like appearance. The market types include PE (Polyethylene), PP (Polypropylene), and PA (Polyamide). PE offers flexibility and moisture resistance, ideal for fresh produce. PP provides high clarity and heat resistance, suitable for ready-to-eat meals. PA offers excellent barrier properties and strength, often used for premium meat and seafood. Each material caters to specific packaging needs, ensuring product freshness and shelf appeal.</p></p>
