<p><p>Automatic Expansion Valves (AEVs) are devices used in refrigeration and air conditioning systems to regulate refrigerant flow based on the cooling load. They ensure consistent evaporator pressure and temperature, optimizing system performance. The market for AEVs is divided into two main types: AEVs without bypass, which directly control refrigerant flow and are suitable for systems with stable loads, and AEVs with bypass, which can accommodate varying loads by allowing some refrigerant to bypass the valve for more flexible control.</p></p>
