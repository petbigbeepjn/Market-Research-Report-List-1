<p><p>The Copper and Copper-alloy Foils market, particularly for thicknesses ≤35 microns, is segmented into two main types: Electrodeposition Copper and Copper Alloys Foil, and Rolling Copper and Copper Alloys Foil. Electrodeposition involves applying a copper layer onto a substrate through an electrochemical process, resulting in uniform thickness and high purity. Rolling foils, on the other hand, are produced by mechanically reducing bulk copper or alloys into thin sheets, offering excellent mechanical properties and flexibility for various applications in electronics and packaging.</p></p>
