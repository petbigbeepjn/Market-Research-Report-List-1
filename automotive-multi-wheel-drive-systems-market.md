<p><p>Automotive multi-wheel drive systems enhance vehicle traction and stability by distributing power to multiple wheels. **Manual Multi-Wheel Drive Systems** require driver intervention to engage or disengage the drive mode, offering greater control and customization for off-road or challenging conditions. **Automatic Multi-Wheel Drive Systems** operate seamlessly, using sensors and algorithms to automatically adjust power distribution based on terrain and driving conditions, providing convenience and optimized performance without driver input.
