<p><ul><li>Fiber laser welding machine</li><li>CO₂ laser welding machine</li><li>Solid-state laser welding machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser welding machine market encompasses three primary types: Fiber laser welding machines, known for their efficiency and precision, utilize fiber optics to deliver high-intensity beams suitable for various materials. CO₂ laser welding machines emit beams through a gas medium, offering versatility for thicker materials but less efficiency on thin ones. Solid-state laser welding machines rely on semiconductor diodes to produce laser light, providing durability and high beam quality, making them ideal for industrial applications. Each type caters to specific welding needs and material compatibility.</p></p>
