<p><strong>The Aerospace TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression) market is categorized into uni-polar and bi-polar types. Uni-polar TVS devices protect sensitive avionics systems from voltage transients by allowing current to flow in one direction, making them suitable for single-ended circuits. In contrast, bi-polar TVS devices can handle voltage spikes in both directions, providing enhanced protection for differential signal lines. Both types are crucial for maintaining the reliability and safety of aerospace electronics in harsh operating conditions.</p></p>
