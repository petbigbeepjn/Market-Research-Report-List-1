<p><ul><li>Absorptive Mufflers</li><li>Reactive Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive muffler market features two primary types: absorptive and reactive mufflers. Absorptive mufflers use sound-absorbing materials to minimize noise by converting sound waves into heat, making them effective for reducing overall engine noise. In contrast, reactive mufflers utilize the principles of sound wave cancellation, employing chambers and baffles to create opposing sound waves that neutralize unwanted noise. Both types aim to enhance vehicle performance and comfort while adhering to regulatory noise standards, appealing to various automotive applications.</p></p>
