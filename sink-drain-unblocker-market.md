Oxidizing unblockers, often containing bleach or similar agents, work by breaking down organic matter through oxidation. Acid unblockers utilize powerful acids to dissolve mineral buildup and tough clogs, particularly effective in metal pipes. Each type targets specific clogging issues and employs distinct chemical mechanisms for effective drainage restoration.</p></p>
