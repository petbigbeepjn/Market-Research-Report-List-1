<p><p>The olefinic thermoplastic elastomers market is primarily divided into two types: thermoplastic polyolefins (TPOs) and thermoplastic vulcanizates (TPVs). TPOs are characterized by their blend of rubber and polypropylene, offering flexibility and durability, making them suitable for automotive and consumer goods applications. TPVs, on the other hand, combine rubber-like properties with thermoplastic processing, providing enhanced elasticity and heat resistance, ideal for use in automotive seals, industrial applications, and consumer products. Both types are valued for their recyclability and performance.</p></p>
