<p><strong>The Antiretroviral Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NNRTIs</li><li>NRTI</li><li>Multiclass Combination Products</li><li>Integrase Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiretroviral therapy market comprises several key classes of medications used to treat HIV. NRTIs (Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors) block reverse transcriptase, preventing viral replication. NNRTIs (Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors) also inhibit reverse transcriptase but through different mechanisms. Integrase inhibitors block the integration of viral DNA into the host genome. Multiclass combination products combine multiple mechanisms into one treatment, enhancing efficacy and adherence. Together, these categories form a comprehensive approach to managing HIV infection.</p></p>
