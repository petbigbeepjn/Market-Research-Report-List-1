Managed Hosting refers to dedicated servers and infrastructure managed by a service provider, offering enhanced performance and support for applications. Managed Colocation allows businesses to house their own servers in a service provider's facility, benefiting from power, cooling, and connectivity without the overhead of building their own data center.</p></p>
