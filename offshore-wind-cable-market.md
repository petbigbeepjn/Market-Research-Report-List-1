<p><strong>The Offshore Wind Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inter-Array Cable</li><li>Export Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore wind cable market primarily consists of two types: inter-array cables and export cables. Inter-array cables connect individual wind turbines within a wind farm, facilitating the transmission of generated electricity to a central point. Export cables, on the other hand, transport the aggregated electricity from the offshore wind farm to the onshore grid. Both cable types are crucial for efficient power distribution, with specific design and material considerations to withstand marine environments and ensure optimal energy transfer.</p></p>
