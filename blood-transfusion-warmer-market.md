<p><p>The blood transfusion warmer market comprises two main types: portable blood warmers and stationary blood heaters. Conversely, stationary blood heaters are fixed units typically found in hospitals or clinical environments, providing continuous heating and specific temperature control for blood transfusions, ensuring patient safety and comfort during procedures.</p></p>
