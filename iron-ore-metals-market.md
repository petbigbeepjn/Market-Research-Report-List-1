<p><p>The iron ore metals market is primarily categorized into hematite, magnetite, and other types. Hematite is the most prevalent iron ore type, known for its high iron content and ease of processing, making it favored in steel production. Magnetite, while lower in iron content, is valued for its magnetic properties and can be upgraded through beneficiation. Other types include limonite and siderite, which are less common and typically have lower iron content, impacting their economic viability in steel manufacturing.</p></p>
