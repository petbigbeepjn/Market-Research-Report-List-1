<p><strong>The Compression Bonded Magnets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonded NdFeB Magnets</li><li>Bonded SmCo Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compression bonded magnets market consists mainly of two types: bonded NdFeB magnets and bonded SmCo magnets. Bonded NdFeB magnets, made from a blend of neodymium, iron, and boron, offer high magnetic strength and are commonly used in applications like motors and sensors. Bonded SmCo magnets, utilizing samarium and cobalt, provide excellent thermal stability and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for demanding environments. Both types are produced using a compression molding process, allowing for complex shapes and precise magnetic properties.</p></p>
