<p><strong>The Magnetic Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Magnetic Bearing</li><li>Passive Magnetic Bearing</li><li>Hybrid Magnetic Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic bearings market comprises three main types: Active Magnetic Bearings (AMBs), Passive Magnetic Bearings (PMBs), and Hybrid Magnetic Bearings. AMBs utilize electromagnetic forces to maintain rotor position, offering precise control and high performance. PMBs rely on the repulsive forces of permanent magnets, providing simpler designs and lower costs but less adaptability. Hybrid Magnetic Bearings combine features of both AMBs and PMBs, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
