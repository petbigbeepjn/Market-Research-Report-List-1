<p><p>Bolus injectors can be categorized into four main types: electronic, mechanical, hydrogel, and hydraulic. Electronic bolus injectors utilize battery-powered systems for precise dosing, making them user-friendly and programmable. Mechanical injectors rely on spring-loaded mechanisms for manual injections, offering simplicity and reliability. Hydrogel injectors use a gel-like substance to enhance drug delivery, optimizing absorption. Hydraulic injectors operate using liquid pressure to administer medication, providing consistent performance. Each type caters to specific medical needs and preferences in drug administration.</p></p>
