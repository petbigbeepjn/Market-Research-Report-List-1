<p><p>The power analyzer market encompasses three primary types: Digital Power Analyzers, Power Quality Analyzers, and Clamp-on Power Analyzers. Digital Power Analyzers measure electrical parameters, providing precise data for energy management. Power Quality Analyzers assess voltage stability, harmonics, and other factors affecting power quality, ensuring optimal performance of electrical systems. Clamp-on Power Analyzers offer a non-intrusive way to measure current and voltage by clamping onto conductors, making them ideal for quick assessments in various applications, including maintenance and troubleshooting.</p></p>
