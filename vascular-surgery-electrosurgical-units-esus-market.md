The market is categorized into three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single electrode to complete a circuit through the patient; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, which utilize two electrodes to minimize thermal spread; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically for sealing blood vessels with precision. Each type offers unique advantages, enhancing surgical efficiency and patient safety in vascular interventions.</p></p>
