<p><p>The geopolymer binder market encompasses several types based on primary raw materials. Slag-based geopolymers utilize industrial byproducts from steel production, offering durability and resistance to environmental factors. Rock-based geopolymers leverage natural minerals, providing unique aesthetics and performance characteristics. Fly ash-based geopolymers are derived from coal combustion byproducts, promoting sustainability by recycling waste. Ferro-sialate-based geopolymers incorporate ferrous materials, enhancing mechanical properties and thermal resistance. Each type serves distinct applications in construction and infrastructure, contributing to eco-friendly building practices.</p></p>
