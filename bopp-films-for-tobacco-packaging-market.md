Non-Shrink films maintain their dimensions and provide barrier protection. Medium Shrink films offer slight shrinkage for better fit and aesthetic appeal. High-Shrink films significantly conform to the product shape, enhancing shelf presence. Printable Shrink films allow for high-quality graphics and branding, making them ideal for marketing while ensuring product integrity.</p></p>
