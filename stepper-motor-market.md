<p><p>The stepper motor market is categorized into three main types: Variable-reluctance (VR), Permanent Magnet (PM), and Hybrid (HB). Variable-reluctance stepper motors operate based on magnetic reluctance changes, offering simple designs and cost-effectiveness. Permanent Magnet stepper motors utilize permanent magnets for better torque and precision, making them suitable for various applications. Hybrid stepper motors combine features of both VR and PM motors, providing high performance and accuracy, making them ideal for advanced applications in robotics, automation, and CNC machinery.</p></p>
