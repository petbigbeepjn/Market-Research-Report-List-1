Passive keyless entry allows for seamless access without traditional keys. Central locking systems enable all doors to lock or unlock simultaneously, increasing convenience. Biometric capture devices provide advanced security through fingerprint or facial recognition, ensuring only authorized users can access the vehicle.</p></p>
