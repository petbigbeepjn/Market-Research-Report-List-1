<p><p>The commercial jet engines market is primarily categorized into three types: turbojet, turbofan, and turboprop engines. Turbojet engines, known for their simplicity and high-speed performance, are primarily used in military aircraft. Turbofan engines, characterized by their efficiency and quieter operation, dominate commercial aviation, balancing speed and fuel economy. Turboprop engines, utilizing a propeller driven by a gas turbine, are ideal for regional flights and shorter distances due to their lower operating costs and excellent performance at lower speeds.</p></p>
