<p><p>Gas-flushing band sealing systems are categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their orientation during operation. Horizontal systems are designed for packaging products lying flat, making them ideal for bulk items and snack foods, as they ensure efficient sealing and continuous flow. Vertical systems, conversely, accommodate products standing upright, suitable for liquids and granular materials, allowing gravity to assist in filling. Both types enhance shelf life and maintain product quality by displacing oxygen and sealing packages effectively.</p></p>
