<p><ul><li>Digital</li><li>Analog</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Universal IO Module market encompasses two primary types: digital and analog modules. Digital modules handle binary signals, enabling precise on/off control for devices such as relays and switches. They are crucial for applications requiring quick and reliable data transfer, like industrial automation. Analog modules, on the other hand, manage continuous signals, measuring variables such as temperature and pressure. These modules are essential for applications needing fine-tuned control and measurement, providing richer information for monitoring and control processes.</p></p>
