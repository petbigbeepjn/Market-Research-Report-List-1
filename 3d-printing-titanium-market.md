<p><p>The 3D printing titanium market includes High Purity Titanium Powder (CPTP) and Alloyed Titanium Powder (ATP). CPTP consists of pure titanium, offering excellent biocompatibility and corrosion resistance, ideal for medical implants and aerospace components. ATP blends titanium with elements like aluminum or vanadium, enhancing strength, durability, and heat resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications in aerospace, automotive, and industrial sectors. Both powders are pivotal in additive manufacturing, enabling lightweight, high-performance, and complex geometries across industries.</p></p>
