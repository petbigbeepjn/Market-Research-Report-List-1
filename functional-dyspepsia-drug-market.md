<p><p>The functional dyspepsia drug market includes various types of medications, primarily focusing on prokinetic drugs and gastric antisecretory drugs. Prokinetic drugs enhance gastrointestinal motility, helping alleviate symptoms like bloating and nausea by speeding up stomach emptying. Gastric antisecretory drugs, on the other hand, reduce stomach acid production, providing relief from heartburn and discomfort associated with excess acidity. Both types play crucial roles in managing functional dyspepsia, catering to different patient needs and symptom profiles.</p></p>
