<p><p>The robotic exoskeletons market is categorized into three types: lower body, upper body, and full body exoskeletons. Lower body exoskeletons assist with mobility, enhancing walking and rehabilitation in individuals with lower limb disabilities. Upper body exoskeletons support arm and shoulder movements, benefiting users in rehabilitation or manual labor. Full body exoskeletons integrate both upper and lower functionalities, providing comprehensive support for mobility and strength, often used in medical rehabilitation, industrial applications, and enhancing physical capabilities of the wearer.</p></p>
