<p><strong>The Pool Salt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rock Salt</li><li>Solar Salt</li><li>Evap Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pool salt market features three primary types: rock salt, solar salt, and evaporated salt. Rock salt is mined from underground deposits and is often less pure, varying in mineral content. Solar salt is produced by evaporating seawater in warm climates, resulting in higher purity levels. Evaporated salt is created by boiling brine, yielding the purest form, ideal for swimming pools. Each type caters to specific needs based on purity, cost, and sourcing, affecting overall water chemistry and maintenance.</p></p>
