<p><strong>The Ultra-High Resolution Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra-High Resolution Camera market is primarily divided into two types: Area Scan Cameras and Line Scan Cameras. Area Scan Cameras capture entire images in a single shot, making them ideal for applications requiring high detail over a wide field of view, such as industrial inspection and surveillance. Conversely, Line Scan Cameras capture images one line at a time, providing high resolution for moving subjects, which is advantageous in applications like web inspection and traffic monitoring. Both types serve specific imaging needs across various industries.</p></p>
