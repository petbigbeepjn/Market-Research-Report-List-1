<p><p>The sway bar bushing market includes three main types: metal, rubber, and polyurethane bushings. Metal bushings offer durability and high strength, ideal for heavy-duty applications but may transmit more vibrations. Rubber bushings provide excellent noise and vibration dampening, commonly used in standard vehicles for comfort. Polyurethane bushings strike a balance, offering improved performance, durability, and reduced flex compared to rubber, making them popular in performance and off-road vehicles. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing strength, comfort, and performance.</p></p>
