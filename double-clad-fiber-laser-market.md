<p><p>The double-clad fiber laser market is segmented into two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers provide a constant output of light, making them ideal for applications requiring sustained energy, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers emit light in short bursts, offering high peak power suitable for precision tasks like marking and engraving. Both types leverage double-clad technology to enhance efficiency and output power, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
