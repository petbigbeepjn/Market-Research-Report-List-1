<p><p>The aramid fiber market is primarily categorized into three types: para-aramid, meta-aramid, and others. Para-aramid fibers, known for their high tensile strength and thermal resistance, are widely used in aerospace, military, and automotive applications. Meta-aramid fibers, offering excellent heat and flame resistance, are predominantly utilized in protective clothing and industrial applications. The "others" category includes specialized aramid fibers that serve niche markets, such as electrical insulation and high-performance composites, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
