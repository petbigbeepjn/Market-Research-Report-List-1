<p><strong>The Home Sleep Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home sleep monitoring equipment market is categorized into two primary types: contact and contactless devices. Contact devices, such as wearable monitors and headbands, physically engage with the user’s body to track sleep patterns through sensors that measure metrics like heart rate and movement. In contrast, contactless devices include non-invasive technologies like sleep tracking mats, cameras, and sound analysis tools, which monitor sleep without direct physical contact, providing insights based on ambient conditions and body language.</p></p>
