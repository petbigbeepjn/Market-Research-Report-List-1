<p><ul><li>Large Particle Composites</li><li>Dispersion Strengthened Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particulate reinforced composites are categorized into two main types: large particle composites and dispersion-strengthened composites. Large particle composites incorporate larger particles that provide enhanced mechanical properties, often resulting in improved toughness and resistance to wear. In contrast, dispersion-strengthened composites utilize smaller particles distributed throughout the matrix to enhance strength and stability without significantly increasing weight. Both types leverage the unique properties of particulate reinforcement to improve performance in various applications, including aerospace, automotive, and construction sectors.</p></p>
