<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photoresist Developer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Developer</li><li>Negative Photoresist Developer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photoresist developer market consists of two main types: positive and negative photoresist developers. Positive photoresist developers remove the exposed areas of photoresist, allowing for intricate pattern creation during the lithography process, ideal for high-resolution applications. Conversely, negative photoresist developers work by removing the unexposed areas, resulting in the remaining photoresist forming the desired pattern or structure. Both types serve critical roles in microfabrication, influencing the performance and precision of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
