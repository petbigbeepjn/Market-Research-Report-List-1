<p><p>The temperature-controlled crystal oscillator (TCXO) market encompasses several types based on output waveforms. Clipped sine TCXOs offer a reduced sine wave suitable for applications needing lower power consumption. CMOS TCXOs provide low-voltage operation and excellent stability, ideal for digital circuits. HCMOS combines high-speed operation with low power, making it versatile for many applications. Sine TCXOs deliver a pure sine wave, ensuring high signal integrity, while other variations may include customized forms targeting specific application needs in telecommunications, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
