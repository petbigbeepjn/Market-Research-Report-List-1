<p><ul><li>OS Containers</li><li>Application Containers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Computer container technology encompasses two primary market types: OS containers and application containers. OS containers, like Docker and LXC, virtualize the operating system to enable multiple isolated environments on a single host, ensuring resource efficiency and faster deployment. Application containers, however, focus on packaging and running software applications along with their dependencies, providing consistent environments across development, testing, and production stages. Both types enhance scalability, streamline DevOps processes, and facilitate microservices architecture, driving the evolution of cloud-based solutions.</p></p>
