<p><p>An Automotive Surround-View System is an advanced driver-assistance technology that provides a 360-degree view of the vehicle's surroundings using multiple cameras strategically placed around the car. This system enhances safety by eliminating blind spots, aiding in parking, and assisting in low-speed maneuvering. It processes real-time video feeds to create a composite bird's-eye view, which is displayed on the vehicle's infotainment screen.</p><p>The Automotive Surround-View System Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for advanced safety features, rising vehicle production, and stricter government regulations on vehicle safety. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14% during the forecast period. Additionally, the rise of electric and autonomous vehicles is further propelling demand for surround-view systems, as these vehicles prioritize advanced safety and driver-assistance features. The Asia-Pacific region is expected to dominate the market due to rapid urbanization, increasing vehicle sales, and growing awareness of road safety.</p></p>
