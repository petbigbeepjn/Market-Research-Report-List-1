The market is segmented into gravimetric QCM, which directly measures mass changes by monitoring frequency shifts, and non-gravimetric QCM, which focuses on other properties like viscosity and acoustic waves to derive data. Both types are utilized in diverse applications, including material science, biosensing, and environmental monitoring, catering to distinct research and industrial needs.</p></p>
