<p><p>The automotive high voltage DC relays market primarily consists of vacuum relays and gas-filled relays. Vacuum relays operate in a sealed environment, offering reliable performance and longevity due to the absence of air, which prevents arcing. Gas-filled relays, on the other hand, utilize a gas medium to enhance electrical insulation and manage heat, thus supporting higher current capacities. Both types are essential in electric and hybrid vehicles for efficient power management, ensuring safe operation of high-voltage systems.</p></p>
