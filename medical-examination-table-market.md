<p><p>The medical examination table market is divided into four main types: manual, hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic. Manual tables require physical effort to adjust their positions, catering to cost-sensitive environments. Hydraulic tables utilize fluid power for height adjustment, offering smooth transitions and ease of use. Electric tables provide powered adjustments, enhancing efficiency and precision, often featuring advanced controls. Pneumatic tables use air pressure for height changes, offering a balance between manual and electric systems, suitable for various healthcare settings.</p></p>
