<p><ul><li>Passive Welding Helmet</li><li>Auto Darkening Welding Helmets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding helmets market comprises two primary types: passive welding helmets and auto-darkening welding helmets. Passive welding helmets feature fixed lenses that provide a consistent protection level but require manual adjustment for different light conditions. In contrast, auto-darkening welding helmets are equipped with sensors that automatically adjust the lens shade in response to the brightness of the welding arc, enhancing convenience and safety. The increasing adoption of auto-darkening helmets reflects advancements in technology, improving user comfort and protection during welding tasks.</p></p>
