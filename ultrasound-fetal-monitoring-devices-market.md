<p><p>The ultrasound fetal monitoring devices market comprises several key types: 2D ultrasound, 3D and 4D ultrasound, and Doppler imaging. 2D ultrasound provides standard flat images of the fetus, primarily used for routine assessments. 3D and 4D ultrasounds offer more detailed, volumetric images, enhancing visualization and allowing real-time fetal movement observation. Doppler imaging focuses on blood flow and heart rate, assisting in evaluating fetal health and potential complications. Together, these technologies enhance prenatal care and maternal-fetal monitoring.</p></p>
