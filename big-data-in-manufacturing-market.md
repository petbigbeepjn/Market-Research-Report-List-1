<p><p>Big Data in manufacturing encompasses three primary market types: discrete manufacturing, process manufacturing, and mixed-mode manufacturing. Discrete manufacturing involves the production of distinct items, like electronics or automotive parts, utilizing data for quality control and efficiency. Process manufacturing focuses on the production of goods that are often liquids or gases, where data informs process optimization and compliance. Mixed-mode manufacturing combines elements of both types, requiring complex data management to streamline operations, enhance flexibility, and drive innovation across varied production methods.</p></p>
