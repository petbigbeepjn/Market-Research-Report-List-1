<p><p>The Polyarylate Fiber market can be categorized into two main types: solid and hollow fibers. Solid polyarylate fibers are characterized by their dense structure, offering high strength, durability, and resistance to heat and chemicals, making them ideal for applications in aerospace, industrial fabrics, and protective clothing. Hollow fibers, on the other hand, feature a tubular structure, providing advantages like lightweight characteristics and enhanced insulation properties. This type is often utilized in applications such as filtration, thermal insulation, and lightweight apparel.</p></p>
