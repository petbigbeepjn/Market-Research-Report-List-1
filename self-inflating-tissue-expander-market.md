Hemisphere-shaped expanders facilitate targeted expansion in specific areas, making them suitable for certain reconstructive applications. Pin-shaped expanders offer precision and control in creating defined tissue expansions, ideal for intricate surgical procedures. Each type serves distinct surgical requirements, enhancing the versatility of tissue expansion techniques in reconstructive and aesthetic surgeries.</p></p>
