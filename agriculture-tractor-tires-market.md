<p><strong>The Agriculture Tractor Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias-ply Tires</li><li>Radial-ply Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agriculture tractor tires market is primarily divided into **bias-ply tires** and **radial-ply tires**. Bias-ply tires are constructed with crisscrossed layers of fabric, offering durability and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks and uneven terrains. Radial-ply tires feature perpendicular layers, providing better fuel efficiency, reduced soil compaction, and enhanced traction, ideal for precision farming. While bias-ply tires are more affordable, radial-ply tires are gaining popularity due to their long-term performance benefits and adaptability to modern agricultural practices. Both types cater to diverse farming needs.</p></p>
