<p><strong>The Wafer Inspection Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Wafer Inspection</li><li>E-beam Wafer Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer inspection machines market comprises primarily two types: optical wafer inspection and e-beam wafer inspection. Optical wafer inspection utilizes light-based technology to detect defects on silicon wafers, offering high-speed and non-invasive analysis, making it suitable for volume production. In contrast, e-beam wafer inspection employs electron beams to achieve high resolution and sensitivity, effectively identifying smaller defects but typically at slower speeds. Both technologies are essential in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
