<p><p>The fusion splicers market is divided into two primary types: single fiber fusion splicers and mass fusion splicers. Single fiber fusion splicers are designed to perform precise joints between individual optical fibers, making them ideal for smaller-scale installations and repairs. In contrast, mass fusion splicers can simultaneously splice multiple fibers, enhancing efficiency for larger projects like telecommunications, data centers, and backbone installations. Both types cater to specific needs within the growing fiber optic networking industry.</p></p>
