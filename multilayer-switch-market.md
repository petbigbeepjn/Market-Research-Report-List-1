<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switch</li><li>Layer 3 Switch</li><li>Layer 4 Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multilayer switch market comprises Layer 2, Layer 3, and Layer 4 switches, each serving distinct functions in network management. Layer 2 switches primarily operate at the data link layer, facilitating local network traffic through MAC address filtering. Layer 3 switches add routing capabilities, enabling inter-VLAN communication and intelligent traffic management at the network layer. Layer 4 switches further enhance performance by incorporating transport layer functions, allowing for load balancing and improved data flow based on application-level information.</p></p>
