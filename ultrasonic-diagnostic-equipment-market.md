<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave Ultrasound Equipment</li><li>Pulsed Wave Ultrasound Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic diagnostic equipment market is primarily segmented into continuous wave ultrasound equipment and pulsed wave ultrasound equipment. Continuous wave ultrasound utilizes a constant sound wave for real-time monitoring, making it suitable for applications like fetal heart rate monitoring. In contrast, pulsed wave ultrasound sends brief pulses of sound, allowing for deeper tissue imaging and a broader range of diagnostic applications. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and expanding healthcare capabilities across various medical fields.</p></p>
