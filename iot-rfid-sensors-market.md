<p><strong>The IoT RFID Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency Sensors</li><li>High Frequency Sensors</li><li>UHF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID sensor market is segmented into three main types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra High Frequency (UHF) sensors. LF sensors, operating at 125-134 kHz, are ideal for short-range applications, providing reliable performance in challenging environments. HF sensors, operating at 13.56 MHz, are commonly used for access control and ticketing due to their higher data transfer rates. UHF sensors, ranging from 300 MHz to 960 MHz, enable longer read ranges and faster data transmission, making them suitable for inventory management and supply chain tracking. Each type caters to distinct use cases based on range, data rate, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
