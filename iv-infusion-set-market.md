<p><p>The IV infusion set market comprises two main types: venting and closed systems. Venting types allow air to enter the IV bag, facilitating fluid flow, making them suitable for certain applications. In contrast, closed types are designed to maintain a sterile environment, preventing contamination and reducing infection risk. Closed systems are increasingly favored in healthcare settings for their safety and efficiency, while venting systems may still be used in specific scenarios. Overall, these variations cater to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
