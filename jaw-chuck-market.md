<p><p>The jaw chuck market comprises three main types: 3-claw, 4-claw, and 6-claw chucks. The 3-claw type is commonly used for quick setups and offers efficient gripping for irregularly shaped workpieces. The 4-claw type provides superior stability and is ideal for cylindrical objects, allowing for better precision and alignment. The 6-claw type is designed for heavy-duty applications, delivering enhanced clamping force and stability for larger, more complex components. Each type serves specific machining needs, catering to various industries.</p></p>
