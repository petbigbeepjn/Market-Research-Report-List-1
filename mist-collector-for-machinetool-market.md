There are three main types: centrifugal mist collectors utilize spinning motion to separate particles from air; electrostatic mist collectors employ electrical charges to attract and capture airborne mist; and media mist collectors use filter media to trap particles as air passes through. Each type offers unique advantages in efficiency and effectiveness, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
