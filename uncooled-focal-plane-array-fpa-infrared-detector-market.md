<p><p>The uncooled focal plane array (FPA) infrared detector market primarily includes two types: Vanadium Oxide (VOx) and Amorphous Silicon (A-Si). VOx detectors leverage the thermoelectric properties of vanadium oxide, offering high sensitivity and resolution, making them suitable for various applications such as security and industrial monitoring. A-Si detectors use amorphous silicon to absorb infrared radiation, providing cost-effective solutions with good performance in commercial applications. Both technologies are pivotal in driving advancements in thermal imaging systems.</p></p>
