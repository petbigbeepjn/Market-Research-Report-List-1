<p><p>The HVAC heat exchanger market includes several types, each designed for specific applications. Shell and tube heat exchangers feature a series of tubes within a shell, allowing for efficient heat transfer between fluids. Plate heat exchangers utilize thin plates for close contact, enhancing heat transfer efficiency. Shell and plate heat exchangers combine the advantages of both designs, providing compact and effective heat exchange. Other market variations may include finned tube, air-cooled, and spiral heat exchangers, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
