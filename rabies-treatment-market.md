CCVs are modern vaccines produced using cultured cells, offering enhanced safety and efficacy. RIGs provide immediate passive immunity by supplying antibodies to individuals exposed to rabies. NTVs, once widely used, are now less common due to safety concerns. Together, these treatments play crucial roles in preventing rabies infection and managing exposure effectively.</p></p>
