<p><p>The plastic colorant market is primarily categorized into three types: inorganic pigments, organic pigments, and dyes. Inorganic pigments, such as titanium dioxide and iron oxides, offer excellent durability and opacity, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Organic pigments are known for their vibrant colors and high tinting strength, widely utilized in consumer goods. Dyes, which dissolve in plastic material, provide transparency and vivid hues. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing aesthetics, performance, and environmental considerations in plastics.</p></p>
