<p><strong>The Linear Low-Density Polyethylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C4- LLDPE</li><li>C6- LLDPE</li><li>C8- LLDPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) market is segmented by its comonomer structure, primarily categorized into C4, C6, and C8 types. C4-LLDPE uses butene as a comonomer, offering flexibility and impact resistance, ideal for films and packaging. C6-LLDPE incorporates hexene, providing enhanced clarity and sealing properties, suitable for various industrial applications. C8-LLDPE, utilizing octene, delivers superior toughness and elasticity, making it preferable for more demanding applications like heavy-duty bags and automotive components. Each type caters to specific needs in packaging, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
