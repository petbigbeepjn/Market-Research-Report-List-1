<p><p>Functional Hot Melt Adhesives (HMAs) are thermoplastic materials that are applied in a molten state and solidify upon cooling, creating strong bonds across various substrates. Common applications span industries such as packaging, automotive, electronics, and construction.</p><p>The Functional Hot Melt Adhesives Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 15% during the forecast period.
