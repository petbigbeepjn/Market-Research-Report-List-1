<p><p>The systemic psoriasis therapeutics market primarily includes biologics and small molecules. Biologics are advanced therapeutics derived from living organisms, targeting specific immune pathways to reduce inflammation and skin lesions effectively. They are typically administered via injection or infusion. Small molecules, on the other hand, are synthetic drugs that can be taken orally, working at the cellular level to modulate immune responses and minimize symptoms. Both types aim to improve the quality of life for patients with systemic psoriasis.</p></p>
