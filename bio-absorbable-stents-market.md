<p><p>The bio-absorbable stents market is primarily categorized into metallic and polymeric stents. Metallic stents, typically made from materials like magnesium or iron, provide structural support and gradually dissolve in the body, reducing long-term complications. Polymeric stents, crafted from biocompatible polymers, offer a more flexible option, allowing for tailored drug delivery while seamlessly integrating with biological tissues. Both types aim to promote healing while minimizing the risk of restenosis, making them essential innovations in cardiovascular treatments.</p></p>
