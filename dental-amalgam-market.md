<p><strong>The Dental Amalgam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-copper Amalgam</li><li>High Copper Amalgam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental amalgam market is primarily categorized into low-copper and high-copper amalgams. Low-copper amalgam contains around 4% copper, making it more prone to corrosion and marginal breakdown. High-copper amalgam, containing 12-30% copper, offers improved resistance to corrosion, better mechanical properties, and reduced likelihood of cavity formation. These distinctions influence their applications in restorative dentistry, affecting patient outcomes, longevity of fillings, and overall preferences among dental professionals, with high-copper amalgams gaining popularity due to their enhanced performance.</p></p>
