<p><p>Gravimetric hygrometers measure moisture content in materials by determining weight changes as water vapor is absorbed or released. The market includes two main types: pointer and digital. Pointer type hygrometers feature analog displays with a needle indicating humidity levels, offering a traditional approach. In contrast, digital hygrometers provide precise readings on electronic displays, enhancing user-friendliness and often including additional features like data logging. Both types cater to various industries, including food processing, pharmaceuticals, and environmental monitoring, ensuring accurate moisture control.</p></p>
