<p><p>Carbon nanotubes (CNTs) are cylindrical nanostructures composed of carbon atoms, known for their exceptional strength, electrical conductivity, and thermal properties. The market for CNTs is primarily divided into two types: Single-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (SWCNTs) and Multi-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (MWCNTs). SWCNTs consist of a single layer of carbon atoms, providing unique electrical properties, while MWCNTs feature multiple layers, offering enhanced mechanical strength. Both types have diverse applications in electronics, materials science, and nanotechnology, driving significant market growth.</p></p>
