<p><p>The seamless steel line pipe market is categorized into three main types: LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) pipes, SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) pipes, and other pipe variants. LSAW pipes feature a straight seam weld and are known for their high strength and performance in high-pressure applications. SSAW pipes, with a spiral weld, are preferred for their flexibility and efficiency in large diameter applications. Other market types may include ERW (Electric Resistance Welded) and seamless pipes, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
