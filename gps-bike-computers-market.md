<p><ul><li>Mapping</li><li>Non-Mapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS bike computer market is divided into two main categories: mapping and non-mapping devices. Mapping bike computers come equipped with detailed navigation features, enabling cyclists to view routes, track locations, and access various terrain information during rides. In contrast, non-mapping bike computers focus primarily on performance metrics such as speed, distance, and cadence, without providing navigational maps. Both types cater to different cyclist needs, with mapping devices preferred for long-distance and off-road adventures, while non-mapping devices suit casual riders seeking basic performance tracking.</p></p>
