<p><strong>The Lead Frames Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stamping Process Lead Frame</li><li>Etching Process Lead Frame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lead frame market comprises two primary types: Stamping Process Lead Frames and Etching Process Lead Frames. Stamping Process Lead Frames are manufactured using mechanical stamping, which is cost-effective and suitable for high-volume production, offering durability and precision. Etching Process Lead Frames, on the other hand, use chemical etching for intricate designs, enabling finer pitches and higher precision, ideal for advanced semiconductor packaging. Both types serve as critical components in IC packaging, with stamping being more economical and etching catering to complex, miniaturized applications.</p></p>
