<p><p>The microparticulated egg white market comprises several types, each tailored for specific applications. Free flow egg whites offer a liquid format that enhances ease of use in cooking and baking. Heat stable egg whites are designed to withstand high temperatures, making them suitable for pasteurization and baking. Gel egg whites provide a thick, gel-like consistency, ideal for desserts and sauces. Whip egg whites are optimized for achieving stable foams, essential in recipes requiring whipped textures, such as mousses and meringues.</p></p>
