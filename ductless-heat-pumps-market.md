<p><p>Ductless heat pumps are available in two primary market types: single zone and multi-zone systems. Single-zone heat pumps are designed to heat or cool individual spaces, offering flexibility and efficiency for small areas, such as single rooms or offices. In contrast, multi-zone systems can simultaneously control the temperature in several areas or rooms, making them ideal for larger homes or buildings. Both types provide energy-efficient climate control without the need for ductwork, enhancing comfort and reducing energy costs.</p></p>
