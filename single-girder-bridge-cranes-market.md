<p><p>The single girder bridge cranes market primarily includes gantry and half-gantry cranes. Gantry cranes are fully supported by legs that allow movement across the workspace, making them ideal for outdoor applications and heavy lifting tasks. In contrast, half-gantry cranes feature one leg and utilize a building's structure for support on the other side, providing flexibility in confined spaces. Both types are essential for efficient material handling in warehouses, manufacturing, and construction industries, showcasing versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
