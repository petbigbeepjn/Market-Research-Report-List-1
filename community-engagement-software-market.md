<p><p>Community engagement software is typically offered in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed directly on a company's servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on the provider's servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and automatic updates. This model reduces the need for extensive IT infrastructure, making it more appealing for organizations seeking flexibility and reduced operational overhead in their community engagement efforts.</p></p>
