<p><p>The pedestrian pallet truck market comprises two main types: manual and electric. Manual pallet trucks require operators to push or pull the load, relying on human effort for movement and lifting. They are ideal for short distances and lighter loads due to their simplicity and lower cost. In contrast, electric pallet trucks are powered, allowing for easier maneuverability and efficiency, especially for heavier loads or longer distances. Both types cater to diverse operational needs in warehouses and retail environments.</p></p>
