<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Seamless Elbow</li><li>Cold Drawn Seamless Elbow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seamless elbow market is categorized into two primary types: hot rolled and cold drawn seamless elbows. Hot rolled seamless elbows are made by heating the material and forming it into shape, offering strength and durability for high-temperature applications. Cold drawn seamless elbows, on the other hand, are produced at room temperature, resulting in tighter tolerances and a smoother finish. Both types are essential in piping systems, serving various industrial sectors, including oil and gas, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
