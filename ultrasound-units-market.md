<p><ul><li>External Ultrasound Units</li><li>Internal Ultrasound Units</li><li>Endoscopic Ultrasound Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound units market is segmented into three main types: external, internal, and endoscopic ultrasound units. External ultrasound units are used for non-invasive imaging on the body’s surface, widely utilized in obstetrics and general diagnostics. Internal ultrasound units, often used in gynecology and urology, involve probes inserted into the body to provide detailed images of internal organs. Endoscopic ultrasound units combine an endoscope with ultrasound technology, allowing for real-time imaging of organs and tissues during minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
