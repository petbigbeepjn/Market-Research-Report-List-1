<p><p>Transcranial Magnetic Stimulators (TMS) are non-invasive devices used for stimulating brain regions through magnetic fields. The market comprises several types: sTMS (static TMS) delivers continuous stimulation, pTMS (peripheral TMS) targets specific brain areas through peripheral stimulation, rTMS (repetitive TMS) employs repeated magnetic pulses to modulate neuronal activity, and nTMS (navigated TMS) uses neuroimaging to precisely target brain regions. Each type has distinct applications in treating neurological and psychiatric disorders, enhancing their therapeutic potential.</p></p>
