<p><p>The In-Mold Coatings market features three primary types: water-based, solvent-based, and powder-based coatings. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, promoting low emissions and easy cleanup, making them ideal for various applications. Solvent-based coatings offer excellent adhesion and durability but may contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Powder-based coatings provide a thick, durable finish without solvents, resulting in minimal waste and overspray. Each type caters to specific industry needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application efficiency.</p></p>
