<p><p>The magnesium raw materials market primarily includes magnesite and brucite, essential for various industrial applications. Magnesite exists in two main forms: phanerocrystalline and cryptocrystalline. Phanerocrystalline magnesite features a coarse crystalline structure, making it suitable for the production of refractory materials and magnesia-based products. In contrast, cryptocrystalline magnesite has a fine-grained structure, preferred for specific applications requiring high purity and reactivity. Brucite, a hydrated magnesium hydroxide, is also utilized in fireproofing and as a filler in various industries.</p></p>
