<p><p>The annatto market comprises three primary types: oil-soluble annatto, water-soluble annatto, and emulsified annatto. Oil-soluble annatto is commonly used in fatty products, providing vibrant color and flavor. Water-soluble annatto is ideal for aqueous formulations, often found in beverages and dairy products. Emulsified annatto combines both properties, making it versatile for use in dressings and sauces. Each type caters to specific industry needs, including food and beverage, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, thereby enhancing product appeal and functionality.</p></p>
