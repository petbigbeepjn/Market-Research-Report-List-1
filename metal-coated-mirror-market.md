<p><strong>The Metal-Coated Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>First Surface Mirror</li><li>Second Surface Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal-coated mirror market primarily consists of two types: first surface mirrors and second surface mirrors. First surface mirrors have the reflective coating applied directly to the front surface, minimizing light distortion and offering superior image clarity, making them ideal for applications like telescopes and lasers. Second surface mirrors, on the other hand, have the reflective coating on the back, resulting in some light distortion but providing cost-effective solutions for general usage, such as in decorative mirrors and basic optical systems.</p></p>
