<p><ul><li>Direct ToF (dToF) Driver IC</li><li>Indirect ToF (dToF) Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automobile Time-of-Flight (ToF) Driver IC market is segmented into two main types: Direct ToF (dToF) and Indirect ToF (iToF) Driver ICs. dToF Driver ICs measure distance directly by calculating the time light takes to return from an object, offering high accuracy and real-time data. In contrast, iToF Driver ICs rely on modulated light signals to infer distance indirectly, typically providing lower resolution but greater range. Each type serves specific automotive applications, enhancing sensor-driven functionalities in vehicles.</p></p>
