<p><strong>The Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake fluid market is primarily segmented into four types: DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 is a glycol-based fluid suitable for standard vehicles, while DOT 4 offers higher boiling points, making it ideal for more demanding applications. DOT 5 is silicone-based, non-hygroscopic, and used in specialized vehicles, but not compatible with other types. DOT 5.1, also glycol-based, combines the benefits of DOT 4 with enhanced performance, ensuring safety across various vehicle types.</p></p>
