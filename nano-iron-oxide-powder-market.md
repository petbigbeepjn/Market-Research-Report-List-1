<p><p>The nano-iron oxide powder market features distinct types, primarily "spherical" and "rod-shaped" forms. In contrast, rod-shaped nano-iron oxide particles possess a high aspect ratio, providing unique magnetic properties and increased surface area, suitable for biomedical applications, magnetic storage devices, and as catalysts. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
