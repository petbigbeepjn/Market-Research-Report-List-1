<p><p>Gym management software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the software from anywhere with an internet connection, offering scalability and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on a gym's hardware, providing more customization and control but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Both types cater to different business needs, influencing cost, accessibility, and operational flexibility for gyms.</p></p>
