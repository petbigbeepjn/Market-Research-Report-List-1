<p><strong>The Wire-cut EDM Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Cut Flushing Type EDM</li><li>Wire-Cut Submerge Type EDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire-cut EDM machines are categorized primarily into two types: Wire-Cut Flushing Type EDM and Wire-Cut Submerge Type EDM. The Wire-Cut Flushing Type features a cooling and flushing system that removes debris during cutting, enhancing precision and speed. In contrast, the Wire-Cut Submerge Type operates in a dielectric fluid bath, offering superior control and reduced thermal effects, resulting in finer surface finishes. Both types serve distinct applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, catering to varying precision and material needs.</p></p>
