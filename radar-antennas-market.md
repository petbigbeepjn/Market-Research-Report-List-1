<p><p>The radar antennas market includes various types catering to diverse applications. Half wave dipole antennas offer broad radiation patterns, suitable for general radar use. Quarter wave dipole antennas have compact designs, ideal for portable systems. Uniform linear array antennas provide customizable beamforming capabilities for improved tracking accuracy. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing radar system performance across defense, aviation, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
