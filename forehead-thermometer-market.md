<p><p>The forehead thermometer market is divided into two main types: non-contact and contact thermometers. Non-contact forehead thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure body temperature without physical contact, offering convenience and reducing cross-contamination risks. They are ideal for quick screenings in public places. In contrast, contact thermometers require direct contact with the skin, usually providing more precise readings but may involve greater hygiene concerns and longer measurement times. Both types serve critical roles in health monitoring and disease prevention.</p></p>
