<p><ul><li>SLI</li><li>AGM</li><li>Lithium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorcycle battery market features three primary types: SLI (Starting, Lighting, Ignition) batteries, AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries, and Lithium batteries. SLI batteries are traditional lead-acid types, ideal for powering motorcycle engines and electrical systems. AGM batteries offer enhanced performance and durability, utilizing a sealed design for maintenance-free use. Lithium batteries are lightweight and provide higher energy density, appealing to performance-focused riders. Each type caters to different rider needs, balancing cost, weight, and performance characteristics.</p></p>
