<p><p>The Natural Film Forming Polymers market is categorized into water-soluble and oil-soluble types, each serving distinct applications. Water-soluble polymers, like gelatin and alginates, dissolve in water, making them ideal for food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, where biodegradability and non-toxicity are essential. Oil-soluble polymers, such as certain resins and waxes, provide hydrophobic properties and are commonly used in coatings, adhesives, and sealants, offering durability and water resistance in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
