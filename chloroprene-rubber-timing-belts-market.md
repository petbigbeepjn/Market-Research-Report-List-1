<p><p>Chloroprene rubber timing belts are essential components in various mechanical applications, primarily characterized by their tooth profiles: trapezoidal and round. Trapezoidal tooth belts have a more angular design that enhances power transmission efficiency and offers greater load-bearing capabilities, making them suitable for high-torque applications. In contrast, round tooth belts are designed for quieter operation and smoother engagement, typically used in lighter-duty systems. Each type addresses specific performance needs, driving their adoption across different sectors, including automotive and industrial machinery.</p></p>
