<p><ul><li>Steering Wheel Assembly</li><li>Turn to Executive Assembly</li><li>Main Controller (ECU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Shift-by-Wire System replaces traditional mechanical linkages with electronic controls for gear shifting. It consists of three main components:  </p><p>1. **Steering Wheel Assembly**: Integrates shift controls (e.g., paddles) for driver input, enhancing convenience and ergonomics.  </p><p>2. **Turn to Executive Assembly**: Converts driver inputs into electronic signals, enabling precise gear changes without mechanical connections.  </p><p>3. **Main Controller (ECU)**: Processes signals, manages gear selection, and ensures seamless communication with the vehicle's transmission system.  </p><p>This system improves efficiency, design flexibility, and driving experience in modern vehicles.</p></p>
