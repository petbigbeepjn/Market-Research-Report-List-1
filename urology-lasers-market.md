<p><strong>The Urology Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid-state Lasers</li><li>Gas-fired Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The urology lasers market features two primary types: solid-state lasers and gas-fired lasers. Solid-state lasers, known for their robustness and efficiency, utilize solid gain media to generate focused light, making them ideal for procedures like lithotripsy and tissue ablation. On the other hand, gas-fired lasers, which use gases such as carbon dioxide or argon, excel in precise cutting and coagulation applications in urology. Both types offer distinct advantages in enhancing surgical precision and patient outcomes in urological treatments.</p></p>
