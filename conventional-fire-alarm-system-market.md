<p><p>The conventional fire alarm system market is categorized into two types based on initiating circuits: One Initiating Circuit and Multi-initiating Circuits. A One Initiating Circuit system features a single activation point, typically suited for smaller buildings or specific areas, ensuring straightforward installation and monitoring. In contrast, Multi-initiating Circuits involve multiple activation points, allowing for extensive coverage and better identification of fire locations. This versatility makes them ideal for larger facilities, enhancing safety and response effectiveness.</p></p>
