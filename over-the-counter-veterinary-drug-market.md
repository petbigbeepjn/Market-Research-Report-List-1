<p><p>The Over-The-Counter Veterinary Drug market is categorized into three main types: topical, oral, and injectable drugs. Topical drugs are applied directly to the skin or mucous membranes to treat local conditions. Oral drugs, administered through food or water, provide systemic treatment for a variety of ailments. Injectable drugs are administered via syringe, offering rapid effects for conditions needing immediate attention. Each type serves distinct therapeutic needs, catering to the diverse range of animal health management and owner preferences.</p></p>
