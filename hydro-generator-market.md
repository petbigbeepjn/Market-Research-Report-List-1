<p><strong>The Hydro Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Turbine</li><li>Reaction Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydro generator market primarily comprises two turbine types: impulse and reaction turbines. Impulse turbines generate power by directing high-velocity water jets onto blades, functioning effectively in high-head applications. In contrast, reaction turbines operate by harnessing the pressure difference in flowing water, utilizing both kinetic and potential energy, making them suitable for low to medium-head applications. Both types cater to diverse energy needs, contributing significantly to sustainable energy generation and enhancing the efficiency of hydroelectric power plants.</p></p>
