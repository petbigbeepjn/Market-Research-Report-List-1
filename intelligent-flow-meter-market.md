<p><strong>The Intelligent Flow Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coriolis</li><li>Magnetic</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Multiphase</li><li>Vortex</li><li>Variable area</li><li>Differential pressure</li><li>Thermal</li><li>Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intelligent flow meter market encompasses various technologies optimized for measuring fluid flow. Coriolis meters measure mass flow based on vibration, while magnetic meters assess volumetric flow using electromagnetic fields. Ultrasonic meters utilize sound waves, and multiphase meters handle multiple phases of fluids simultaneously. Vortex meters detect vortices created by obstructions, and variable area meters measure flow through a changing cross-section. Differential pressure meters gauge flow based on pressure drop, thermal meters measure heat transfer, and turbine meters use rotational flow for measurement.</p></p>
