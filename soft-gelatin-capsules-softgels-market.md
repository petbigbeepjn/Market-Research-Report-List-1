<p><p>The soft gelatin capsules (softgels) market is segmented into two main types: gelatin-based and non-animal-based capsules. Gelatin-type softgels are made from animal-derived gelatin, commonly sourced from bovine or porcine, and are widely used due to their excellent bioavailability and stability. In contrast, non-animal type softgels utilize plant-based materials, such as hydroxypropyl methylcellulose (HPMC), catering to vegetarian and vegan consumers. This segmentation allows companies to target diverse consumer preferences and dietary restrictions, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
