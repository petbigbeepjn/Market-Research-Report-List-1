<p><p>The Pharmaceuticals Glass Vials market includes three primary types: Type I, Type II, and Type III glass. Type I glass is borosilicate and offers superior chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for storing sensitive formulations. Type II glass is treated soda-lime glass that provides moderate protection, often used for less sensitive drugs. Type III glass is standard soda-lime glass with basic properties, suitable for more stable formulations. Each type serves distinct needs in the pharmaceutical industry, ensuring product integrity and safety.</p></p>
