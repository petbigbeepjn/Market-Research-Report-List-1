<p><strong>The Industrial Plastic Granulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beside-the-Press Granulators</li><li>Central Granulators</li><li>Heavy-Duty Granulators</li><li>Thermoforming Granulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial plastic granulators market features several key types, each designed for specific applications. Beside-the-press granulators operate next to injection molding machines, efficiently recycling sprues and scraps. Central granulators handle larger volumes and are used for bulk waste management. Heavy-duty granulators are built for tough materials and high-output processing, suitable for industrial recycling. Lastly, thermoforming granulators are specialized for granulating waste from thermoforming processes, ensuring material reusability in manufacturing. Together, these devices cater to diverse plastic recycling needs.</p></p>
