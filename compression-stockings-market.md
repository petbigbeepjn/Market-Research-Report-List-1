<p><ul><li>Gradient Socks</li><li>Anti-Embolism Socks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compression stockings serve various healthcare needs, primarily categorized into gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks apply varying pressure levels, greatest at the ankle and decreasing up the leg, promoting blood flow and preventing conditions like varicose veins. Anti-embolism socks, designed for immobile patients, provide consistent pressure to reduce the risk of deep vein thrombosis (DVT). Both types play crucial roles in enhancing circulation, particularly during surgery, long flights, or for individuals with circulation issues.</p></p>
