<p><ul><li>Jet Air Type</li><li>Warm Air Dryer (WAD) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fully automatic electric hand dryer market comprises two main types: Jet Air Type and Warm Air Dryer (WAD) Type. Jet Air Dryers utilize high-speed jets of air to rapidly evaporate moisture from hands, offering quick drying times and energy efficiency. In contrast, Warm Air Dryers provide a gentler airflow with warmer air, drying hands at a slower pace but often being quieter and less intense. Both types prioritize hygiene and convenience in public restrooms and high-traffic areas.</p></p>
