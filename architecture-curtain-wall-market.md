<p><p>An architectural curtain wall is a non-structural outer covering of a building, typically made of lightweight materials such as glass, metal, or stone. It is designed to protect the building from external elements while providing aesthetic appeal and energy efficiency. The integration of renewable energy solutions, such as photovoltaic glass, is also gaining traction, further driving market growth.</p></p>
