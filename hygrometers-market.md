<p><p>The hygrometers market comprises various types, including capacitive, resistive, thermal, and gravimetric hygrometers. Capacitive hygrometers measure humidity via changes in electrical capacitance. Resistive types assess humidity based on changes in electrical resistance within materials. Thermal hygrometers utilize temperature variations caused by moisture, while gravimetric hygrometers determine humidity through weight changes in moisture-absorbent materials. Additionally, "others" encompasses specialized or emerging technologies. Each type serves distinct applications based on accuracy, sensitivity, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
