<p><strong>The Free Radical Photoinitiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cracking Photoinitiator</li><li>Hydrogen Capture Photoinitiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Free Radical Photoinitiator market is primarily divided into two types: Cracking Photoinitiators and Hydrogen Capture Photoinitiators. Cracking Photoinitiators facilitate the generation of free radicals by undergoing bond cleavage upon exposure to light, enabling polymerization processes in coatings and adhesives. In contrast, Hydrogen Capture Photoinitiators are designed to capture hydrogen to form reactive species, enhancing the efficiency of the polymerization. Both types play critical roles in various applications, including UV curing and photo-initiated polymer processes.</p></p>
