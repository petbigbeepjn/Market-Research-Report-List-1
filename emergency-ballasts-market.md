<p><ul><li>Magnetic Emergency Ballasts</li><li>Electronic Emergency Ballasts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency ballasts market is primarily categorized into two types: magnetic and electronic emergency ballasts. Magnetic emergency ballasts utilize traditional electromagnetic technology, providing reliable backup power during outages but often with lower efficiency and longer response times. In contrast, electronic emergency ballasts employ advanced circuitry, allowing for quicker activation, higher energy efficiency, and dimming capabilities. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in commercial and residential settings, enabling lights to function during power failures.</p></p>
