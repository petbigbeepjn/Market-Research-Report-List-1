<p><p>The Medical Equipment Cooling System market comprises two main types: liquid-based and air-based cooling systems. Liquid-based cooling systems utilize refrigerants or cooling fluids to effectively manage heat in high-performance medical devices, ensuring optimal operation and longevity. In contrast, air-based cooling systems rely on airflow to dissipate heat, typically using fans or natural convection. While liquid systems offer superior cooling efficiency, air systems are often simpler and cost-effective, catering to diverse medical equipment needs and operational environments.</p></p>
