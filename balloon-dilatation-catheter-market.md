<p><p>A Balloon Dilatation Catheter is a medical device used primarily in procedures to widen narrowed or obstructed blood vessels and passages in the body. Often utilized in angioplasty, the catheter consists of a thin, flexible tube with an inflatable balloon at its tip. When the balloon is deployed, it expands under controlled pressure, effectively compressing plaque against the vessel walls and enlarging the lumen to restore normal blood flow.</p><p>The Balloon Dilatation Catheter Market is experiencing substantial growth, driven by increasing incidences of cardiovascular diseases, rising awareness of minimally invasive procedures, and advancements in catheter technology. The demand for effective treatment options in interventional cardiology is propelling market expansion.
