The market features three main types: horizontal closed tanks, which offer efficient space utilization and are often easier to clean; silo or vertical closed tanks, which provide high capacity and are suitable for larger dairies; and open tanks, which are cost-effective and simple but less effective in insulation and hygiene. Each type serves varying operational needs based on farm size, production volume, and specific cooling requirements.</p></p>
