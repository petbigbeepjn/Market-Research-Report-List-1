<p><p>The solar cells and modules market comprises various technologies catering to diverse energy needs. Mono-Si modules offer high efficiency and space savings through single-crystal silica. Multi-Si modules are popular for their balance of cost and efficiency, utilizing multiple silicon crystals. CdTe modules leverage cadmium telluride for cost-effective production, while CIGS modules use copper indium gallium selenide for flexibility and lightweight applications. A-Si modules, based on amorphous silicon, are ideal for low-light conditions. Other technologies include novel and hybrid solutions expanding the market further.</p></p>
