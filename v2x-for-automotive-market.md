<p><p>V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) encompasses various communication types enhancing automotive safety and efficiency. V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle) allows vehicles to share information, reducing collisions by alerting drivers to nearby hazards. V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure) connects vehicles with road infrastructure, enabling traffic signal coordination and real-time updates. V2P (Vehicle-to-Pedestrian) improves pedestrian safety by alerting vehicles of nearby foot traffic. Other markets include V2N (Vehicle-to-Network) for cloud connectivity and V2G (Vehicle-to-Grid) for energy management, contributing to smarter transportation systems.</p></p>
