<p><strong>The Fibre Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode Fibre</li><li>Single-mode Fibre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fibre optics market is primarily divided into two types: multimode fibre and single-mode fibre. Multimode fibre consists of multiple light paths, allowing for higher data transmission over short distances, making it ideal for local area networks and data centers. In contrast, single-mode fibre has a single light path that enables long-distance communication with higher bandwidth and less signal loss, suitable for telecommunications and long-haul applications. Both types support the growing demand for faster and more reliable data connectivity.</p></p>
