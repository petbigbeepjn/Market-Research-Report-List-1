<p><strong>The Shunt Reactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Immersed</li><li>Air-Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shunt reactor market is primarily categorized into two types: oil-immersed and air-core reactors. Oil-immersed shunt reactors, featuring a transformer-like construction, use insulating oil to enhance cooling and prevent overheating, making them suitable for high-voltage applications. In contrast, air-core reactors utilize air as an insulating medium, offering lighter weight and easier installation. Both types serve to manage reactive power and voltage stability in electrical grids, ensuring efficient energy transmission and improved reliability.</p></p>
