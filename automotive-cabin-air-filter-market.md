<p><p>The automotive cabin air filter market is primarily divided into two types: particle automotive cabin air filters and charcoal automotive cabin air filters. Particle filters are designed to capture dust, pollen, and other airborne particles, ensuring a cleaner cabin atmosphere. In contrast, charcoal filters incorporate activated charcoal, effectively removing odors and harmful gases, such as volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Both types play crucial roles in enhancing air quality within vehicles, contributing to passenger comfort and safety.</p></p>
