<p><p>The Large Flow Ultrapure Water System market encompasses two primary technologies: Reverse Osmosis (RO) combined with Ion Exchange (IX), and Reverse Osmosis coupled with Electrodeionization (EDI). RO+IX systems effectively remove dissolved solids and impurities, ensuring high water quality, while IX adds further purification by removing specific ions. In contrast, RO+EDI systems utilize continuous electrodes to achieve high levels of ultrapure water production with reduced chemical usage. Both technologies cater to industries requiring stringent water quality standards, such as pharmaceuticals and electronics.</p></p>
