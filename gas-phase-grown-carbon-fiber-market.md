<p><ul><li>PAN Based</li><li>Asphalt Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas phase grown carbon fiber market is primarily divided into PAN (polyacrylonitrile) based and asphalt-based segments. PAN-based fibers are known for their superior mechanical properties and are commonly used in aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods applications, providing high strength and modulus. In contrast, asphalt-based carbon fibers are typically more cost-effective and find use in industrial applications, offering good thermal and electrical conductivity but generally lower performance compared to PAN-based fibers. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
