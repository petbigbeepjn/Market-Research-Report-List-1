<p><p>The small oil sealed pumps market includes various types, each catering to specific applications. Rotary vane pumps utilize rotating vanes to create a vacuum and are known for their efficiency in low-pressure applications. Screw pumps operate using helical screws, providing smooth and continuous flow, ideal for transferring viscous fluids. Piston pumps rely on reciprocating pistons to generate pressure, suitable for high-pressure scenarios. Together, these pump types offer diverse solutions for industries requiring reliable and efficient oil sealing mechanisms.</p></p>
