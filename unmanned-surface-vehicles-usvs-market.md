<p><p>Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs) are autonomous boats used for various applications like surveillance, oceanographic research, and environmental monitoring. The market includes two primary types: wave-powered and propeller-driven USVs. Wave-powered USVs harness ocean energy for propulsion, making them efficient and environmentally friendly. In contrast, propeller-driven USVs utilize conventional propulsion systems, providing speed and maneuverability. Both types cater to diverse operational needs, with wave-powered models often focusing on long-duration missions and propeller-driven units excelling in rapid response scenarios.</p></p>
