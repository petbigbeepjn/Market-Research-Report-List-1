<p><p>The Light Goods Conveyor Market comprises various types catering to different material handling needs. Drag chain conveyors utilize a chain system to move items, suitable for heavy loads. Roller conveyors, available in driven or gravity variants, facilitate efficient movement with minimal manual effort. Plastic type conveyors are lightweight, offering flexibility and resistance to corrosion, ideal for diverse applications. Slat type conveyors utilize slats to support products, providing stability for transporting bulky or irregular-shaped items effectively across production lines.</p></p>
