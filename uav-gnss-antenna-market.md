<p><p>The UAV GNSS antenna market features various types, including helix and patch antennas. Helix antennas are known for their circular polarization and ability to maintain signal integrity across varying altitudes and orientations, making them suitable for dynamic UAV applications. Patch antennas, on the other hand, are compact and lightweight, offering a low-profile solution for GPS and GNSS applications. Both types cater to specific needs in the UAV sector, enhancing positioning accuracy and reliability for navigation and data collection tasks.</p></p>
