<p><p>The cable car and ropeways market includes various types designed for transporting passengers and materials. Aerial tramways consist of large cabins suspended from cables, providing scenic views. Chairlifts feature open chairs that transport skiers and hikers. Gondolas have enclosed cabins for comfort and weather protection. Surface lifts, like tow ropes, help skiers ascend slopes while remaining on the ground. Material ropeways are used for transporting goods and materials across challenging terrain, making them vital for industries in remote locations.</p></p>
