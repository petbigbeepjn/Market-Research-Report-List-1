<p><p>The lithographic photomasks market is categorized into several types based on materials and applications. Quartz masks are high-precision masks made from quartz glass, offering excellent transmittance and thermal stability, ideal for advanced semiconductor fabrication. Soda lime glass masks are more economical but less effective for critical applications. Film masks are often used for lower-resolution applications due to their flexibility and lower cost. Additionally, "Others" includes specialized materials or prototypes catering to niche markets within the lithography process.</p></p>
