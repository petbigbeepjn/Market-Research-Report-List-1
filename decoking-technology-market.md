<p><strong>The Decoking Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Decoking Systems</li><li>Closed Coke Slurry System (CCSS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Decoking technology encompasses methods used to enhance the efficiency of coking processes in petroleum refineries. Hydraulic Decoking Systems utilize high-pressure water jets to dislodge and remove coke deposits from furnace tubes, improving throughput and operational safety. Conversely, Closed Coke Slurry Systems (CCSS) capture coke in a closed loop, allowing for better handling and reduction of emissions. Both technologies aim to optimize maintenance, enhance productivity, and minimize environmental impact, making them vital in modern refining operations.</p></p>
