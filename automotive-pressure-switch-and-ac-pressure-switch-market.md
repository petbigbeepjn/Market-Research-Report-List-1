<p><p>The automotive pressure switch market includes various types designed for specific functions in vehicle systems. Dual function pressure switches manage both high and low pressure, enhancing efficiency. High-Pressure Cut Off (HPCO) switches protect the AC compressor by shutting off at dangerous pressure levels. Fan switches control the cooling fan operation based on system pressure. Compressor cycling switches regulate the compressor’s operation, maintaining optimal performance and preventing damage by cycling it on and off according to pressure variations.</p></p>
