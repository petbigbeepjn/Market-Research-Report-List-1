<p><p>The passenger lifts market primarily consists of two main types: hydraulic and electric passenger lifts. Hydraulic lifts operate using a hydraulic system, offering smooth and quiet movement, ideal for low-rise buildings. They are known for their reliability and ease of maintenance. In contrast, electric passenger lifts utilize a counterweight system, providing faster and energy-efficient transport, suitable for mid to high-rise buildings. Both types cater to different architectural needs and user preferences, ensuring safe and efficient vertical transportation.</p></p>
