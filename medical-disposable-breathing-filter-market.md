<p><p>The medical disposable breathing filter market includes two main types: mechanical filters and electrostatic filters. Mechanical filters primarily rely on physical barriers to trap particles and contaminants from inhaled air, ensuring cleaner airflow. In contrast, electrostatic filters use an electrical charge to attract and capture pollutants, enhancing filtration efficiency. Both types are critical for preventing the transmission of pathogens during respiratory procedures, thereby improving patient safety and supporting infection control in healthcare settings.</p></p>
