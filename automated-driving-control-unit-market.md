<p><strong>The Automated Driving Control Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perception Control Unit</li><li>Decision Control Unit</li><li>Actuator Control Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Driving Control Unit market is segmented into three key types: Perception Control Unit, Decision Control Unit, and Actuator Control Unit. The Perception Control Unit processes data from sensors to understand the environment. The Decision Control Unit analyzes this data to make driving decisions, such as navigation and obstacle avoidance. Finally, the Actuator Control Unit translates these decisions into actions, controlling the vehicle's steering, acceleration, and braking systems. Together, they enable safe and efficient automated driving.</p></p>
