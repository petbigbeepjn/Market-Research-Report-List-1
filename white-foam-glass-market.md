<p><strong>The White Foam Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The White Foam Glass market is divided into two primary types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell foam glass features tightly packed cells that are sealed, providing excellent insulation and moisture resistance, making it ideal for construction and refrigeration applications. In contrast, open cell foam glass has interconnected pores, which allow for better sound absorption and flexibility in design. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries, catering to specific needs in insulation, lightweight construction, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
