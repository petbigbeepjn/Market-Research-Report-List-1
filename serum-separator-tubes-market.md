<p><p>Serum Separator Tubes (SSTs) are specialized medical devices used for the collection and separation of serum from blood samples. These tubes contain a gel barrier that allows for the efficient separation of serum from cellular components during centrifugation. SSTs are predominantly utilized in clinical laboratories for various diagnostic tests, including biochemical assays and immunological studies, due to their ability to yield high-quality serum samples with minimal contamination.</p><p>The Serum Separator Tubes Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.50% during the forecast period. The trend towards automation in laboratories, along with innovations in tube design and materials, is further enhancing the efficiency and reliability of serum collection and processing.
