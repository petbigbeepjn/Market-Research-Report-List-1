<p><p>The 3D Interactive Lens and Assemblies Market comprises various technologies, primarily focusing on Structured Light Method and Time of Flight (ToF) Method. The Structured Light Method uses a projector to cast patterns onto a surface, capturing depth information by analyzing deformations in the light. In contrast, Time of Flight Method measures the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back, allowing for precise distance calculation. Both methods enhance applications in gaming, robotics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
