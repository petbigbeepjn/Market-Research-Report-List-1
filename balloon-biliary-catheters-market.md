<p><strong>The Balloon Biliary Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Balloon Catheter</li><li>Cutting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Scoring Balloon Catheter</li><li>Drug Eluting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Stent Graft Balloon Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Balloon biliary catheters are crucial in treating obstructive biliary conditions. Normal balloon catheters are used for dilation, while cutting balloon catheters have micro-blades to incise strictures for better expansion. Scoring balloon catheters feature scored surfaces that facilitate controlled dilation. Drug-eluting balloon catheters release therapeutic agents to prevent restenosis, enhancing treatment efficacy. Stent graft balloon catheters are designed to deploy stents during dilation, providing structural support to the bile ducts. Together, these types cater to diverse therapeutic needs in biliary interventions.</p></p>
