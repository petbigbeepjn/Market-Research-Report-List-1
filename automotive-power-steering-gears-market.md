<p><p>The automotive power steering gears market comprises three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electric Power Steering (EPS), and Other systems. HPS utilizes hydraulic fluid to amplify steering effort, offering responsive handling but requiring engine power. EPS, on the other hand, uses electric motors to assist steering, improving fuel efficiency and reducing weight. "Other" includes emerging technologies like steer-by-wire systems, which enhance precision and customization. Collectively, these types cater to diverse vehicle designs and driver preferences.</p></p>
