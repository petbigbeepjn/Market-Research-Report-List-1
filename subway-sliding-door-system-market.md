<p><ul><li>Spindle</li><li>Toothbelt Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Subway Sliding Door System market primarily comprises two types: Spindle and Toothbelt units. Spindle units utilize a rotating spindle mechanism to facilitate smooth door movement, offering robust durability and reliability. Conversely, Toothbelt units operate through a toothed belt system, ensuring seamless and quieter door operation, ideal for high-frequency use. Both systems are designed to enhance passenger safety and accessibility while maintaining efficient space management in subway stations, contributing to the overall effectiveness of public transportation infrastructure.</p></p>
