<p><p>The smart plug market is primarily segmented into Bluetooth and Wi-Fi types. They are ideal for small spaces and low-energy appliances. In contrast, Wi-Fi smart plugs provide broader connectivity, allowing remote access via the internet. This enables users to control devices from anywhere, often integrating with smart home ecosystems and voice assistants, making them more versatile for comprehensive home automation.</p></p>
