<p><p>The smart smoke alarm market offers various types of detectors catering to different fire scenarios. Photoelectric alarms utilize a light source to detect smoke via light scattering, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization alarms feature radioactive material that senses fast-flaming fires through ion flow disruption. Dual sensor alarms combine both technologies, offering comprehensive protection against all fire types. Each type emphasizes advanced features, connectivity, and smart technology integration, enhancing safety and providing real-time alerts to users.</p></p>
