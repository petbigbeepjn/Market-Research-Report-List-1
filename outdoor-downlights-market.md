<p><p>The outdoor downlights market features three main types: recessed, surface-mounted, and pendant lights. Recessed downlights are installed into ceilings for a sleek, streamlined appearance, ideal for modern designs. Surface-mounted downlights are mounted directly onto surfaces, offering versatility in installation while providing direct lighting. Pendant lights hang from overhead fixtures, adding aesthetic appeal and ambient illumination to outdoor spaces. Together, these types cater to various architectural styles and functional needs, enhancing outdoor environments with both form and function.</p></p>
