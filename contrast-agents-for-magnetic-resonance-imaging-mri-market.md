<p><p>Contrast agents for MRI enhance image clarity and diagnostic accuracy in medical imaging. Barium-based contrast media are primarily used in gastrointestinal imaging, improving visibility of the digestive tract. Iodinated contrast media are utilized in CT scans but have limited MRI applications. Gadolinium-based contrast agents are the most common for MRI, providing excellent soft tissue contrast. Microbubble contrast media are employed for ultrasound imaging, enhancing the visualization of blood flow and vascular structures, offering a complementary imaging approach.</p></p>
