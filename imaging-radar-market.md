<p><strong>The Imaging Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Millimeter-Wave Imaging Radar</li><li>Laser Imaging Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Millimeter-Wave Imaging Radar and Laser Imaging Radar are advanced sensing technologies used in various applications. Millimeter-Wave Imaging Radar operates in the 30-300 GHz frequency range, offering high-resolution imaging through obstacles like fog, dust, and walls, making it ideal for security, automotive, and industrial applications. Laser Imaging Radar, or LiDAR, uses laser pulses to create precise 3D maps, excelling in autonomous vehicles, robotics, and environmental monitoring. Both technologies provide non-contact, high-accuracy imaging but differ in wavelength, range, and environmental adaptability.</p></p>
